# Dragon Kisser

This is a year-long video game capstone project for my Computer Science program at my university that started April 2025 and is slated to finish in April 2026. It's built in Unreal Engine 5 and our team consists of roughly 30 students including artists, programmers, and designers.

This project is currently in progress and is set to be finished April 2026.
