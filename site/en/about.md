# About Me

I am an aspiring game developer with a focus on engine architecture, gameplay programming, and game design. I enjoy performance programming and optimization. 

While programming is my main skill, I spent a lot of time throughout all my projects developing my technical art skills, including character modeling, rigging, and animation. This has helped me understand the limitations and interactions between design, art, and mechanics. My goal is to become a well-rounded developer that understands all stages of video game development pipelines.

### How I Work

More than anything else, I am a team player. I love working with tight-knit teams towards a common goal or project. I’ve learned through many group projects that I am a leader. During one specific project in college (<a href="/projects/en/metroid-brimstone/" class="accent">Metroid Brimstone</a>), I led a group of 3 other college students to make a fully flushed out video game prototype with a complete game loop and final assets. At the close of this project, my teammates told me that it was the single most fun group project they’ve ever worked on as students.

#### <a class="accent">Video games are built to be played, but they're also a form of art. This means the development process is often just as important as the end user experience.</a>
